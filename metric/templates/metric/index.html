Informed healthcare. Peace of mind.

Patients has the right to know what level of healthcare services it receives in each health center. Today, the cost of healthcare services are vastly different among health centers and informed patients will not be frustrated while receiving health care services. The level of frustration in the case of emergency shall be managed more effectively.

Oh yeah, potential benefits. See for yourself.

Measures such as quality of care have direct impact on on factors such as additional visits to health center, less complications, and wrong diagnosis. Patients can receive more affordable health services with similar quality of care using own insurance. Given the abundance of data it is possible to determine patients diagnosis group from sympotms.
